Sarah Hajjar

Sarah Hajjar is one of the two deuteragonists and traveling companions of Amir. She is the daughter of Uthman Hajjar and Fahima Hajjar. Even though Sarah is called a treasure of Aintidina, she has a two sided personality with the sweet side being a facade.

Appearance

Sarah is a young, thin girl. She has an almond skin tone and long, violet tinted hair. Her eyes have a purple hue. Her attire always changes, cause she wants to be completely immersed in the country. She also has a mole under her right eye.

Personality

Sarah has a double sided personality. When not alone with Azhar or Amir she acts kind and forgiving, but when she isn't with strangers her ego gets larger and she becomes bold, smug and prideful. She also loves influence and wealth.

Background

Sarah was born in Aintidina. After a few years the village made her out to be girl that would become the kindest and most beautiful woman in all of Alsahriqa, at first she really liked those claims, but after growing up it became a burden. Her uncle, a merchant, was one of the only people that didn't treat her differently, so she felt at home with him. She sometimes went to the big city together with him to pass time away from the village, there she picked up the skill of trading and negotiating.

Relationships

Amir

The name Amir has been given to him by Sarah Hajjar, she acts like a dependable and mature big sister figure that overly spoils him, although it isn't even sure that she's older than him. At first Amir lets her do anything she wants to him and also follows her around, but later on he resists a bit more. He also copies what she says once in a while which ends with Azhar getting harassed by not only Sarah, but also Amir. She is travelling with Amir and Azhar.

Azhar Hajjar

Azhar is the older brother of Sarah. When they were younger Azhar used to follow around his sister, this was cause of him being 'the village's treasure, elder brother' and trying to live up to their expectations. They are close enough for Sarah to act normal around him and they also bicker. After setting sail with Amir, Azhar begins to talk back to Sarah and corrects her. He also teaches her basic spearmanship and tool usage.

Equipment

Polearm

A spear with a medium to small spearhead and an extra horizontal blade next to the tip.

Bag

A bag that almost always contains the money pouch of trio, firefly lantern, roll of cloth, rope coil, 2x water pouch, 5x small knives, machete + ecology, biology, geology, random and booksRandom purchases.

Abilities & Conditions

Spear

Sarah wields a spear with a dagger horizontally attached to the spearhead, which she uses to stab and pull enemies. She doesn't practice any professional martial arts, but has Azhar help her with her technique and flaws. She uses a combination of hand to hand combat and her spear. She mostly uses her momentum to her advantage instead of professional techniques.

Spirit

Sarah's spirit allows her to create illusions. She summons two giant eyes, the eyes go to opposite locations and allow Sarah to create illusions in whatever is in both their line of sights, later on she can distort multiple senses at a time and make bigger changes.
