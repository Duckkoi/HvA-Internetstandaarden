Azhar Hajjar

Azhar Hajjar is one of the two deuteragonists and traveling companions of Amir. He is the son of Uthman Hajjar and Fahima Hajjar. Azhar looks like a very serious and menacing person, but he isn't anything like that.

Appearance

Azhar is a tall and well-built guy. He has short dark purple tinted hair and eyes which have a dark blue and purple mix. Just like his sister he has an almond skin tone. His eyes are sharp and thin. Cause of sister his attire also changes according to the norms of the settlement or area.

Personality

People generally get the wrong impression of Azhar, thinking he's aggressive and sketchy, even though he's actually nothing like that. Azhar is the most understanding and sympathizing out of the main characters, there are a few people including Sarah which he doesn't act as friendly with. When in a new settlement one of the first things he does is look at the female inhabitants, for multiple reasons.

Background

Since young he has been tasked with monitoring and protecting his sister, Sarah. The members of their village looked at Sarah as a treasure and thought of Azhar as her guard. At first he found it a bit annoying, but quickly got used to his role. His days consisted only of following Sarah and then spending time with Hasib Dagher, which he viewed as his tutor. Most of the knowledge Azhar has comes from Hasib, for example spear techniques and reading. One day while following Sarah, he realizes what she's really like, this is when they open up to each other and start bickering.

Relationships

Amir

When out in the wild Amir always listens to Azhar. This is cause of Azhar being older, he also asks a lot of questions to Azhar about how to do stuff like fishing, cause even though he has the memories, he wants to have a 'correct' way. Amir comes to him for approval of most things and even later on this doesn't change. They have a very healthy relationship.

Sarah Hajjar

Sarah's initial impression of Azhar wasn't really clear, all she though was that he was a pain since she couldn't be alone anywhere with him following her. After Azhar catches Sarah's real personality he was a bit weirded out, but it made it easier for Sarah to be herself. Now Sarah and Azhar bicker a lot, but they can still work together, though not if it comes to Amir since they both want different things from him.

Equipment

Polearm

A halberd with a long head and a hook on its head. He mostly uses his halberd to fling people around. His spearmanship is a mix of what he learned in Alsahriqa and experience in the outside world.

Bag

A big bag that almost always contains a roll of cloth, rope coil, fishing net, fishing rod, sack of grubs, machete, large rope net, large cloth, 2x water pouch, 4x throwing spear, Sarah's random purchases.
